Visual Story Book Design

Resources: Design process blog.

Overview: This is a print design project. I was supposed to design a book for print of a certain thesis. I wanted to design a children's story book based on the story of me and my dog. My target audience was kids, so I focused more on the visuals than on the texts.

Design process: I chose square size and used bright colors and vector arts for the visuals of the book. I wrote the texts as if my dog was telling the story. The design choices were based on the premise that the book was for children. More details on the book can be found on my Medium blog (link in Resources).
