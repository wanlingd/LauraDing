EmpowerU Website Redesign

Resources: Live website; Advertising video.

Overview: EmpowerU (formerly known as DWEP) is a non-profit organization that aims at empowering domestic workers in Hong Kong and other regions of Asia. It provides education and support for the domestic workers in an affordable way. This website displays the detailed information about EmpowerU.

Note: EmpowerU rebranded in late 2018. This website is a redesign of the old DWEP website.

User Research: Users for this website include domestic workers and potential investors. However, due to lack of funding and other limitations, only domestic workers participated in the research. I also researched other similar websites to learn from them.

Design Process: Based on the research results, I created prototypes and then designed the website. Then I sent the website to the person who was the direct contact of the domestic workers and asked for feedback and iterated the design.
