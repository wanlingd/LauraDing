Poster Series Design

Resources: Design process blog.

Overview: This is a print design project. I was supposed to design three posters for an event. My event was a music festival called 'Sober'. The idea I wanted to convey through this event and my design was that you don't have to be drunk to enjoy the music. The thesis of my event was energetic and dreamy.

Design process: I made nine iterations of poster designs. Because this is a print design assignment, I improved my design based feedback from my peers and instructors. Details about the design process can be found on my Medium blog (link in Resources).
